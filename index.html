Our Story

A Southern Trinidad
Institution

Nestled along the Southern Main Road in La Romain, Zaza's has been the go-to destination for premium grilled dining in South Trinidad. What started as a passion for perfectly cooked meats has grown into one of the region's most beloved dining experiences.

Every cut is sourced with care, every sauce crafted in-house, and every dish prepared to order. From our signature tenderloin to our celebrated Bajan Fish Fry, each plate tells a story of quality and love.

A proudly Halaal establishment — family-friendly, no alcohol, no smoking — where the whole community comes together around the grill.
